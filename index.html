We know how this sounds.

An ad inside your AI tools sounds like the start of a bad story. So we made Lull impossible to distrust: the entire codebase is open source and auditable on GitHub. Lull never reads your prompts, your code, or your responses — the only thing it detects is that a request started and when it finished. Ads are targeted at no one, because we have nothing to target with. Everything is opt-in: you choose which tools Lull runs in, and turning it off is one click. If you ever find a line of code that contradicts this, we'll have a much bigger problem than your uninstall.

Questions

How much will I actually earn?

Honestly: coffee money, not rent. Heavy AI users currently make a few dollars a month. As the network grows, so does your share — we'll publish averages publicly so you're never guessing.

What does Lull see?

Nothing you type. Lull detects only the loading state of supported tools — never prompts, code, or responses. The source is public; don't take our word for it.

Which tools are supported?

VS Code and Cursor via the IDE plugin — that's the current v0.1 scope. Browser support (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) is on the roadmap and ships next.

How do payouts work?

USDC to any wallet, or Stripe straight to your bank. Cash out anytime once you pass $10. No points, no gift cards, no lock-ins.
